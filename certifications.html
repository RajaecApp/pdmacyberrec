Certifications

Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)

The CISSP certification is a globally recognized credential that validates an individual's expertise in information security.

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)

The CEH certification demonstrates an individual's skills in identifying and addressing security vulnerabilities through ethical hacking techniques.

CompTIA Security+

The CompTIA Security+ certification covers foundational cybersecurity skills, including network security, risk management, and compliance.
